Shelfswap · v0 · wireframes

Core screens,
low fidelity.

Seven screens covering the whole loop — build a library, find a book, propose a swap, agree a handoff, close it out. Real copy, deliberately unpolished visuals. The goal is to argue about structure, not colors.
